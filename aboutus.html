ABOUT US

The Animal Welfare Awareness initiative is managed by the Department of Veterinary Services Malaysia (DVS) as part of its continuous efforts to promote responsible animal care and strengthen animal welfare practices. Providing awareness and education to the public, especially pet owners, is one of the important responsibilities under DVS to encourage proper animal management, protection, and compassionate treatment towards animals.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leading veterinary authority in ensuring animal health, welfare and public well-being.

Mission

To protect animal health and welfare through effective services, sustainable practices and public awareness.
